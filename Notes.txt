Dwarf dug too deep and punctured a lava vein.  Climb upwards to escape the mines before they are flooded with lava.

Powers:
Pick upgrade (break stronger blocks)
Climbing Gloves (grab corners and ledges)
Steel Booots (Stomp down)
Firestone (Lights up darkness)
Grappeling Hook (Drawn to ceiling.  Resets on touching floor)
